Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?

We’re open Monday to Friday from 8 AM to 6 PM, and on weekends from 9 AM to 5 PM.

Do you offer any non-dairy milk options?

Yes, we offer almond, oat, and soy milk options for our drinks.

Can I order online?

Yes, we have online ordering available through our website and our partnered delivery services.

What types of coffee do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of coffee options, including espresso, cappuccino, latte, mocha, drip coffee, and more. Our menu caters to all tastes and preferences.

Do you offer any loyalty or rewards programs?

Yes, we’re excited to offer a loyalty rewards program! Every time you make a purchase, you earn points that can be redeemed for discounts, free drinks, and exclusive offers. Sign up in-store or online to start earning rewards on your next visit!.
